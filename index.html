INTRODUCTION

Pepul is a community-driven, Frog-themed NFT collection. It is a collection of 10,000 unique and high-quality NFTs. PEPUL is a combination of the word PEPU and People indicating that we are the true PEPU Community.

PEPUL is a movement that shows how strong PEPU community is. The NFT can be used as an avatar and it is a reward & a proof of participation on our movement.

ETH profit from the collection will then be used to buyback and burn PEPU tokens.

Now, let's use our spare change to support our ecosystem.

NFT PROFIT ALLOCATION

50% - of the earnings will be used to buyback and burn PEPU tokens.
